Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard the data you provide through our website.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal details—such as your name, email address, company and message—when you voluntarily submit them via our contact form or email.

We do not collect or store Protected Health Information (PHI). Please do not include any medical or patient information in your messages.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiries and deliver our services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics and Cookies

We may use privacy‑friendly analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. These tools collect anonymous usage data and do not track you across websites. We do not use advertising cookies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page. By using this site, you agree to the terms outlined here.
